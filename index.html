About me

Aspirations

Hi! My name is Deb and I am an aspiring developer. I am currently enrolled in a coding bootcamp called Altcademy and am in the process of completing their Full-stack Web Development program. The curriculum covers HTML/CSS, JavaScript, React, Ruby on Rails and computer science fundamentals. Following the completion of the program, I am looking to build full-stack applications and prototypes.

Professional experience

I previously worked as an investment professional with two leading technology venture capital funds (VantagePoint Capital Partners, Tandem Expansion Fund) and have also held strategy and product development roles with leading FinTech firms (GAIN Capital, WorldFirst). Following a decade in business roles, I've decided I'd like to become a developer as I love building new things.

Formal education

I previously completed a master's degree in electronic commerce and internet computing (a hybrid business/engineering degree) at the University of Hong Kong and studied finance at McGill University.

Portfolio

Bootstrap 4


In this project, I was asked to clone Airbnb's press page from scratch. I was required to use Bootstrap 4 and Flexboxes while minimizing custom CSS to the maximum extent possible. The project was a lot of fun as it demonstrated the power of Bootstrap 4 to develop prototypes quickly. I also learned the power of using high quality images and spacing to improve the user experience.
